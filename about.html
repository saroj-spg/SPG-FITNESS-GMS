Strategies

Start slowly and build up gradually. Give yourself plenty of time to warm up and cool down with easy walking or gentle stretching. Break things up if necessary. Be creative, listen to your body, and stay flexible.

Workout

Aim for a well-rounded fitness plan that includes aerobic fitness, strength training, core exercises, balance training, and flexibility. Adding these elements to your regular routine can help you achieve lifelong fitness.
